Algorithms & Equations

Gravity causes sand to saltate and create ripples. The height of the sand after saltating at x and y position equal to the sand before saltating at x and y positon minus the amount of sand being moved at x and y positon. A more in-depth explation of this phenomenon is that the height of the sand after saltating at x position plus the distance that the sand moves horizontally plus the distance that the sand moves vertically is equal to the sand before saltating at x position plus the distance that the sand moves horizontally plus the distance that the sand moves vertically minus the amount of sand being moved at x and y positon.
